Backstory 🖋
...

  • We are a few days from Neverwinter, where we left
  • We are in the employ of Gundrun, who has tasked us with bringing some supplies from Neverwinter
  • We will be paid 5GP each upon delivery of the provisions to Phandalin

Dead Horses 💀🐴
...

  • We come upon two dead horses blocking the path
  • There are black arrows sticking out of the horses
  • These are arrows from the Cragmaw Goblin tribe
  • Ivor sneaks ahead to investigate and find 4 goblins hiding in ambush

Combat⚔❗
...

  • A catapult launches a large turtle shell covered in spikes that knocks Bor out
  • Goblins are killed by magic missiles, bell tolls, and weapons
  • We win the combat!

Combat Over
...

  • The shell has a dragon tooth sticking out of it
  • Dagan tries to pull it out but can't
  • He tries to smash the shell with his hammer and fails

The Light on the Goblin
...

  • Amber sees a glowing light that is vibrating in the pocket of a goblin
  • Amber determines it is not arcane
  • She calls Dagan over to look at it
  • Dagan pulls it out and finds a large orange spike:
    • It is 12 inches long
    • It goes to a pointy end
    • It's about two hands around at the wide end
    • Dagan can tell it came from the shell that crushed Bor
    • We reattach the spike to the shell!

Return of Thudd
...

  • Thudd comes back to life and his weight crushes and kills Bor
  • Amberdite detects his thoughts and determines he is not looking to fight us
  • Thudd introduces himself
  • Marco tells him he can join us for some gold
  • We start digging a hole to burn Bor's body in
  • Thudd tells Amberdite of how he resurrects every now and then
    • Last thing he remembers in being a huge reptile's mouth
  • Dagan gives an inspiring speech
  • We stop for a short rest

Following the Trail
...

  • Tracks tell of two human sized people being dragged away
  • We set off following the tracks
  • Ivor spots a bear trap
  • We decide to go around it
  • About 10m later Ivor spots what appears to be a concealed pit
  • Dagan remembers what he can of goblins and reminds everyone to watch for ambushes and traps

Discovering the Cave
...

  • Our trail brings us to a cave with a creek flowing out of it
  • Ivor spots two goblins before they see him
  • We ambush them and kill them without issue
